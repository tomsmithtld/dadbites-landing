Why I built this

My partner went back to work and I was on solo feeding duty. I had no idea what was safe for a 7 month old. Is avocado ok? What about eggs? Can he have bread yet?

I spent more time googling than actually feeding the kid. Every site had different advice. Some were clearly wrong.

DadBites pulls from NHS and WHO weaning guidelines. Not mum blogs, not AI-generated content. Actual guidance that paediatricians would agree with. All in one place, searchable in 2 seconds.
